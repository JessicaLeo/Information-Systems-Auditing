System Analysis:

50% of confirmed data breaches targeted small businesses with fewer than 1,000 employees. 92% of the businesses who had experienced a data breach said they lost personally identifiable information such as Drivers Licenses, Credit Cards or Social Security Numbers. 89% of breaches and data loss incidents could have been prevented. For smaller businesses, the worst cyber breaches cost between $84,000–$148,000. 60% of small businesses fail within 6 months of a cyber attack. 89% of breaches and data loss incidents could have been prevented. 83% say they don’t have a plan in place for keeping their businesses cybersecure.
